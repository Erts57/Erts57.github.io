UNO: SINGLEPLAYER MODE

How to Play:

You start with 7 cards, each card has a number or a symbol on it. There is also a deck, next to it there is the discard pile. Clicking on a card with the same color or symbol with adding it to the discard pile. Your goal is to use up all your cards until you have no more.

Special Cards:

Plus 2 Card: Adds 2 cards.
Reverse Card: Changes random cards color.
Skip Card: Removes a random card.
Wild Card: Counts as every color.
Plus 4 Card: Counts as every color & adds 4 cards.
